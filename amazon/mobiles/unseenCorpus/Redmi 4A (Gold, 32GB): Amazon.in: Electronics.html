From the manufacturer

Power Beyond a Day

Redmi 4A is powered by the large-capacity 3120mAh battery and coupled with system-level MIUI optimizations. It guarantees up to an average 7-day standby time. So now you can embark on movie marathons, launch apps and play games without worrying about running out of battery.

Read more

Qualcomm Snapdragon 425 64-bit Processor

Expect fast app launches and fluid gaming experience on Redmi 4A, packed with Qualcomm Snapdragon 425. The 64-bit, quad-core processor is filled with system-level optimizations that can manage multiple tasks simultaneously. It comes with 128GB of expandable memory to house all your music, movies and more.

Read more



Captures Picture Perfect Moments

Take gorgeous group photos and scenic shots on the new Redmi 4A. It comes with a 13MP camera that captures sharp and crisp photos. MIUI intelligently classifies them based on date and is even able to detect if it is a panoramic shot or a screenshot. So now you have a handy companion to help you take, edit, and save those memories that are meant to be kept forever.

12.6cm (5) HD Display

Redmi 4A has a 5" fully laminated display packed with pixels for HD resolution. It also has Reading mode, which prevents eye fatigue by reducing blue light rays from screen. Color temperature can also be adjusted based on your preference - warm, standard or cool.

131.5g is the New Light

Despite its large capacity battery, Redmi 4A stays amazingly slim and light at a mere 131.5g. It is housed in a seamless, polycarbonate body that boasts a super smooth matte finish and is till date, the lightest in the Redmi series.

Read more



Simplify your Life, MIUI 8

Be in awe whenever you pick up your Redmi 4A. It runs on MIUI8 - which is the most gorgeous and user-friendly operating system. We've included some major design and software overhauls that will help you to be even more efficient in your daily life. You can now take scrolling screenshots, calculate currency conversions, create dual app log-ins and expect more as we roll out more version updates in the future.

One Single Phone, Dual Identities

Redmi 4A allows you to keep 2 separate profiles. Use different passcodes to access them, each with its own wallpaper, apps, files and photos. Perfect for times when you need a clear separation in your life.

Faster Networking with 4G

Redmi 4A comes with 4G connectivity for faster networking, downloading and VoLTE support.
