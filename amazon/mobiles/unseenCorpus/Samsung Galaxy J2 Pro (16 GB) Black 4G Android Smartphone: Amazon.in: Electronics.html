Product description

The attractive chrome bezel, appealing matte finish and the stylish patterned back, defines the attractive and seamless design of the new Samsung Galaxy J2 Pro (16 GB). Carrying few of the Samsung's most innovating features i.e. Smart Glow (Next Generation LED Notification System), S-Bike Mode and the efficient Turbo Speed Technology (TST), Samsung J2 Pro is undoubtedly a must buy. It is a 4G enabled Dual-SIM smartphone with 2 GB RAM, 1.5 GHz Quad-Core Processor and the 12.7 cm (5) sAMOLED display, which offers the complete world of infotainment at your fingertips. Swipe the screen to unlock it, prioritize notifications into simple categories and enjoy seamless browsing & smooth navigation - the New UI (User Interface) in Samsung J2 Pro has much more to explore. Adding more, the Ultra Saving Mode adds to its optimum performance and the OTG Support function helps in its convenient usage. This smart mobile phone by Samsung promises you a non-stop fun over your phone. Be it multi-tasking, connectivity, web browsing, gaming or movie watching, the new Samsung J2 Pro 2016 Edition is designed to offer you an efficient and hassle-free performance. Incorporating the latest Marshmallow Operating System (OS), this Android 6.0 smartphone comes along with 1 year Samsung India warranty.
